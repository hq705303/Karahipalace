Our Heritage

Authentic Flavors,
Made to Order

Karahi Palace is Laurel's destination for bold, authentic Pakistani cuisine. Every dish is made fresh to order using time-honored recipes, certified Halal meats, and hand-selected spices that honor the rich culinary traditions of Pakistan.

From our rich tomato-based karahis to our charcoal-grilled kabobs and hearty Nehari, every plate is crafted with passion, tradition, and love. We are proudly Halal — every day, without exception.
