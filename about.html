Our Story

Born from Passion,
Built on Tradition

Karahi Palace is Laurel, Maryland's destination for bold, authentic Pakistani cuisine. Our story began with a simple but powerful vision: to bring families and communities together over food that truly feels like home.

Every dish we serve is made fresh to order using certified Halal meats, freshly ground spices, and time-honored recipes rooted in Pakistani culinary tradition. From our rich tomato-based karahis to our charcoal-grilled kabobs, every plate is crafted with care.

It's more than just a meal — it's a taste of tradition and heritage on every plate.

We proudly offer Dine-In, Carryout, and Catering services. All specialties are made to order — please call ahead of time. We appreciate your patience and trust.

Day One

Our Halal Promise

At Karahi Palace, the Halal commitment is absolute. Every piece of meat we serve is sourced from certified Halal suppliers. We are a completely alcohol-free establishment — every day, without exception.

This is more than a business standard — it is a matter of trust with our community. Every Muslim family, individual, and guest can dine at Karahi Palace with complete confidence and peace of mind.
